Symptoms of Mental Distress

If you experience any of these symptoms, click on "help" immediately for support and guidance. Your well-being matters, and seeking help is the first step toward feeling better..

Persistent sadness or mood changes
Intense anxiety or worry
Difficulty coping with stress
Changes in sleep or appetite
Social withdrawal
Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
